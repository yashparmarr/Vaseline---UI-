VASELINE® JELLY 101

What is a skincare occlusive moisturizer?

An occlusive moisturizer forms a physical barrier on the top layer of our skin that prevents moisture loss. Occlusives seal in the water, so it doesn't evaporate. It makes skin feel soft and smooth and can also relieve irritation like itching or burning. The degree of occlusivity of Petrolatum Jelly is unique as it gives effective protection and moisturization, while aiding barrier recovery.

Is Vasline® Jelly safe for skin?

Vaseline® ensures the safety of its petroleum jelly by putting it through a triple purification process. Vaseline® Jelly has been around for over a century and is actually less irritating to skin than water! It's clinically proven to heal and protect your skin.

What does the Triple Purified Seal mean?

Our seal guarantees every jar of Vaseline® is 100% contaminant-free. Vaseline® Jelly is purified three times which exceeds the US purity standards. Another reason you can trust Vaseline®.

Does Vaseline® Jelly cause acne?

Vaseline® Jelly is non-comedogenic, which means it doesn’t clog pores in the skin.
