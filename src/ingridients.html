OUR ORIGINAL HERO INGREDIENT: VASELINE® PETROLEUM JELLY

Vaseline® Jelly has been around for over 150 years. It's the original skin protectant and an effective occlusive that seals in moisture and helps the skin's natural healing process.

ONE INGREDIENT, MULTIPLE BENEFITS

Petroleum jelly's unique properties accelerate the skin's barrier recovery, melting into the spaces between skin cells providing superior occlusivity. Vaseline® Jelly is hypoallergenic, non-comedogenic, non-irritating and held to the highest purity standards. It’s 100% pure, and a great ingredient for locking in moisture. That's why so many people reach for it to heal their dry skin.

This amazing ingredient has been approved to be used on cracked heels and feet, dry hands and cuticles, dry and itchy skin, chapped lips, and more. Its applications are endless!

HOW VASELINE® JELLY WORKS, AND WHY IT’S SO AMAZING.

Petroleum jelly is a versatile ingredient with many skin benefits, including a unique skin barrier recovery mechanism, making it the perfect skincare occlusive. It essentially offers effective protection and moisturization, while promoting skin barrier recovery. How? It penetrates the skin layers and then re-crystallizes, replacing lipids and boosting the skin's ability to repair itself. It not only protects but also helps your skin’s healing process. People don’t call Vaseline® Jelly the “Wonder Jelly” for nothing!

VASELINE® JELLY 101

What is a skincare occlusive moisturizer?

An occlusive moisturizer forms a physical barrier on the top layer of our skin that prevents moisture loss. Occlusives seal in the water, so it doesn't evaporate. It makes skin feel soft and smooth and can also relieve irritation like itching or burning. The degree of occlusivity of Petrolatum Jelly is unique as it gives effective protection and moisturization, while aiding barrier recovery.
