SKIN HEALTH FOR ALL

We believe skin health is essential for wellbeing. And we're on a mission to give everybody, everywhere access to the skin care they need.

VASELINE® EXISTS TO GIVE EVERY BODY HEALTHY SKIN

Our skin goes wherever we go and moves with us through life. It’s the only one we get. That’s why Vaseline® is committed to understanding and supporting skin health for all types of skin, all walks of life. So you can live without limits.

TRUSTED & EFFECTIVE

HEALTHY SKIN LEADS TO BEAUTIFUL SKIN

Vaseline’s® skin health credentials stem from the original Vaseline® Jelly, which sits at the center of our product portfolio and also at the center of our social mission program, The Vaseline® Healing Project. Every product we make promotes healthy skin either by healing, protecting, or reversing and preventing skin dryness. Whether it's Vaseline® Protecting Jelly to help with minor scrapes or Vaseline® Derma Care Advance Repair Lotion to soothe very dry skin, we create highly researched, scientific formulas that everyone can use.Our product ranges include products that moisturize skin to make it more resilient and give you that natural, healthy-looking glow

Skin Health For All

Vaseline® is on a mission to give access to healthy skin to every one, everywhere. Whether you are a young professional in Bangkok worried about pollution and sun exposure, an eczema sufferer in the US, or a family displaced by a natural disaster in the Philippines, we're committed to help care for and support the skin health of every body.

THE VASELINE® HEALING PROJECT:

SKIN HEALING FOR THOSE IN NEED

We not only believe in "Skin Health for All", we put those words into action. In 2015, in partnership with Direct Relief, we created The Vaseline® Healing Project to provide dermatological care, Vaseline® Healing Jelly, and medical supplies to people living in areas with acute need or affected by natural disasters worldwide. We also provide training for dermatologists in places with limited access to skin health care. We believe healthy skin is a right, not a privilege. And that's why we're bron a mission to give everyone, everywhere beautiful, healthy skin.
