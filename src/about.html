HEALTHY SKIN LEADS TO
BEAUTIFUL SKIN

Vaseline’s® skin health credentials stem from the original
Vaseline® Jelly, which sits at the center of our product portfolio
and also at the center of our social mission program, The
Vaseline® Healing Project. Every product we make promotes healthy skin either by healing, protecting, or reversing and preventing skin dryness. Whether it's Vaseline® Protecting Jelly to help with minor scrapes or Vaseline® Derma Care Advance Repair Lotion to soothe very dry skin, we create highly researched, scientific formulas that everyone can use. Our product ranges include products that moisturize skin to make it more resilient and give you that natural, healthy-looking glow
